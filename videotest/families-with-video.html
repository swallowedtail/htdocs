HAND HOLDERS

We've thought of loads of little ways to make your trip with little ones as easy as possible, watch this video to find out how.

ALL ABOARD

If you’re travelling with under 5s, we'll board you and your family early.

BRING THE ESSENTIALS

You can bring all the food and drink your baby needs for the journey. Just bring the baby milk in powdered form and we’ll provide the hot water.

NAPPY CHANGING

Facilities for nappy changing are provided on all our flights.

CABIN BAGS FOR KIDS

Children sitting in their own seat can bring one cabin bag on board to fill with their favourite things, just like grown-ups.

SITTING TOGETHER

If you booked a child seat, we do all we can to make sure you sit together. But if you want to be certain that you are, you can choose your seats from just £XX*.

BUGGIES TO THE BOARDING GATE

You can take two travel items for children with you to the boarding gate, such as buggies, car seats, push chairs and travel cots. For more information please visit our website.

 

*Prices may vary, refer to our website for more details.
